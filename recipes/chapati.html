Chapati(Indian flatbread)

Description

Chapati is an unleavened Indian flatbread made with just three ingredients: whole wheat flour, ghee or oil, and water. It's soft, thin, and perfect for scooping up curries or enjoying on its own.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour (atta)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup water
  • Wheat flour for rolling and dusting

Steps

  1. Mix whole wheat flour, salt, and water to form a soft dough.
  2. Divide the dough into portions and roll each portion into a round chapati.
  3. Cook the chapati on a hot tava (griddle) until it puffs up and gets brown spots.
  4. Serve with your favorite curries or use it for wraps and rolls.
